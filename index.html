SERVICES

Laboratory Testing

Once the blood samples are collected, the agency will perform various laboratory tests on the samples. These tests can include a wide range of analyses, such as complete blood count (CBC), lipid profile, liver function tests, kidney function tests, glucose levels, hormone levels, infectious disease testing, genetic testing, and many others.

Result Interpretation

After the laboratory testing is complete, the agency will provide a detailed report of the test results. They may also offer interpretation services where a healthcare professional can explain the significance of the results and answer any related questions.

Flexible Appointments

Our professional phlebotomists provide reliable and hygienic home blood collection services, ensuring accurate and trustworthy results. With flexible appointment scheduling and the convenience of having your blood samples collected in the comfort of your own home, we make the process hassle-free and accessible for you.
