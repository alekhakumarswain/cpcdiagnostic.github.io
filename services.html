Our Services

Blood Tests

We offer a wide range of blood tests to help diagnose and monitor various medical conditions. Some of the most common blood tests we perform include:

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Blood Glucose Test
  • Lipid Panel
  • Liver Function Test
  • Rapid Flu Test
  • Thyroid Function Test

Before your blood test, you may be required to fast for a certain period of time or avoid certain foods or medications. Your healthcare provider will give you specific instructions.

Diagnostic Imaging

We use state-of-the-art imaging equipment to help diagnose and monitor various medical conditions. Some of the most common types of imaging we perform include:

  • X-ray
  • Computed Tomography (CT) scan
  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)
  • Ultrasound

Your healthcare provider will give you specific instructions on how to prepare for your imaging appointment, such as fasting or avoiding certain foods or medications.

Other Procedures

In addition to blood tests and imaging, we offer a variety of other procedures to help diagnose and monitor medical conditions. Some of the most common procedures we perform include:

  • Electrocardiogram (EKG)
  • Pulmonary Function Test (PFT)
  • Spirometry
  • Stress Test

Your healthcare provider will give you specific instructions on how to prepare for your procedure.
